Welcome

We Love Dogs as Much as You Do

At Happy Tails, every dog is family. We understand that leaving your furry best friend can be stressful — that's why we've built a warm, caring environment where dogs thrive, play, and receive the individual attention they deserve.

Our certified team of passionate dog lovers is trained in animal first aid, behaviour, and nutrition — so you can go about your day knowing your pup is in the very best hands.
